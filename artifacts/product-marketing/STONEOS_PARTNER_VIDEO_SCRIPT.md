## Accuracy note

The partner demo mode is query-gated and uses fictional operational, customer and financial values. Predictive outputs are presented as transparent operational decision support derived from current workflow states, not as guaranteed machine-learning outcomes. The receiving, cutting, LPM and sales forms shown in screenshots were populated for capture but not submitted.
